#iKeepMyResolutions

A challenge that helps people around the world achieve 2019 resolutions. Follow the simple rules below in order to participate.

Join challenge

1. Make a resolution NOW

No need to wait until New Year's eve to start achieving your goals. Check out these ideas for inspiration and make a resolution today.

2. Commit publicly to keep it

Leave a comment in the reddit thread or use the #iKeepMyResolutions hashtag on instagram, facebook, twitter, etc. You don't have to reveal the resolution itself.

3. Track the progress

Each week write a few words on how well you're doing. It will help you stick to the resolution. You can subscribe to get weekly reminders to track progress.

4. Help others to achieve their resolutions

Tell your friends about this challenge. If they accept it, occasionally ask about the progress in order to help them keep the resolutions.
