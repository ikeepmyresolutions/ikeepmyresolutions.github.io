#iKeepMyResolutions

A challenge that will help you achieve your New Year's resolutions. Follow the 4 simple rules below in order to participate.

Join challenge

1. Make a resolution (or a few ones)

Prepare resolutions in advance, keep them realistic and set specific goals. Check out these ideas for inspiration.

2. Commit publicly to keep it

Use the #iKeepMyResolutions hashtag on instagram/facebook/twitter (you don't have to reveal the resolution itself) or leave a comment in a relevant reddit thread.

3. Track the progress

At the end of each week or month write a few words on how well you're doing. It will help you stick to the resolutions.

4. Challenge your friends

It's more fun to achieve New Year's resolutions together!
